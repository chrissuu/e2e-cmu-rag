Baseball's brightest stars will shine on July 14 at the 2026 MLB All-Star Game presented by Mastercard at Citizens Bank Park in Philadelphia, PA. The 96th Midsummer Classic follows MLB All-Star Week favorites such as Capital One All-Star Village, the All-Star Futures Game, the MLB All-Star Celebrity Softball Game, Gatorade All-Star Workout Day and the T-Mobile Home Run Derby.
