Nemacolin Woodlands Resort
Situated on 2,000 acres in the Laurel Highlands of Southwestern PA, nothing compares to Nemacolin Woodlands Resort. Complimentary experiences include an indoor pool, whirlpool, eucalyptus lavender-infused steam and sauna. A long list of prestigious salon and spa services includes a Five Elements Journey treatment, Immersion packages for ultimate relaxation and seasonally specific experiences.
The only warning is that a treatment at Nemacolin Woodlands Resort Spa may spoil future spa experiences anywhere else.
