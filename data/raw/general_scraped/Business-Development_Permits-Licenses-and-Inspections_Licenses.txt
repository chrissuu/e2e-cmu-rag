All licenses expire 365 days from the date of issuance. License holders who allow their license to expire will be charged a late fee after the date of expiration. Late fee grace periods are provided for all trade and contractor licenses. Please see the links above for more information on your license type and late fees.
The format of all required Certificates of Insurance documents should be the following. COIs not formatted in this way will not be accepted: The name insured should be the owner’s name and the business/operating name should be shown under “DBA,” if applicable. Tax compliance and COI should have exactly the same owner’s name and DBA operating/business name. The address in the Certificate Holder field should read: City of Pittsburgh 412 Blvd of the Allies PLI, Floor 3 Pittsburgh, PA 15219
