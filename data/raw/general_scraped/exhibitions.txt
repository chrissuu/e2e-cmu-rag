French Moderns
From June 20 - Oct. 11, 2026, The Frick Pittsburgh presents French Moderns. Encompassing realism, impressionism, post-impressionism, symbolism, Fauvism, cubism and surrealism, French Moderns exemplifies the avant-garde movements that defined modern art in the nineteenth and twentieth centuries and that continue to awe art-goers today.
