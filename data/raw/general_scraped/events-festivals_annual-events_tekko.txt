Tekko will run from July 23-26, 2026 at the David L. Lawrence Convention Center, which is located at 1000 Fort Duquesne Boulevard .
Parking
David L. Lawrence Convention Center offers a spacious parking garage on 10th Street with rates ranging from $8 to $30. There are also three bike rack stations in the garage, which are situated on level one and the northside exterior by Fort Duquesne Boulevard.
