Duo Two Ways
Oct 17, 2025 - Oct 17, 2025																   | 9:00 pm - 10:30 pm
Arcade Comedy Theater 943 Liberty Avenue Pittsburgh, PA 15222
Watch veteran improvisers Fred Brown and Scott Pettis perform two very different improv styles. First, the form known as the "TJ & Dave" – patient, theatrical, naturalistic. Second, the "Zumpf" – fast, bold, always moving. Whether you're simply looking for an entertaining show or a dedicated improv nerd, this show will leave you thinking, "Wow."
