Publication Archives
Carnegie Mellon News
Carnegie Mellon News , published from 1993 to 2003, contained stories of interest to the campus community, university news, research, sports highlights and more. Issues from 2001 to 2003 are available at www.cmu.edu/cmnews/ (opens in new window) .
Carnegie Mellon Magazine
Published from 1982 to 2003, Carnegie Mellon Magazine contained news briefs of campus happenings, feature stories about the university and alumni, and an extensive Class Notes section. The magazine was primarily for the campus community and university alumni. Issues from 2002 and 2003 are available at www.cmu.edu/magazine (opens in new window) .
