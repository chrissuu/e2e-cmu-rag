When & Where
The 2024 JFilm Festival is May 2-12, 2024 and is being held at Carnegie Music Hall – Oakland, McConomy Auditorium - Carnegie Mellon University, The Oaks Theater, and virtually in Pennsylvania, West Virginia, and Ohio.
Admission
Opening reception tickets, single showing tickets, virtual tickets, and festival passes are available for purchase on the JFilm website. Tickets will also be available for purchase at the venue 30 minutes prior to showtime.
Purchase Tickets Online
Accessibility
All venues are wheelchair accessible and ASL or audio description is available upon request with advance notice. Please note: The Oaks Theater does not have an accessible restroom but one is available at Fox’s Pizza, the restaurant next door.
