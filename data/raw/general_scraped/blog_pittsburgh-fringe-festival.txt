Connect with Fellow Art Enthusiasts with a Fringe Button
Looking for new ways to dive into Pittsburgh’s arts community? Fringe buttons aim to spark conversations between event goers, discussing specific shows and performances during the festival. Visitors who are purchasing tickets have the option to receive a three dollar discount on admission with a button. Support the Fringe Festival by buying a button and bonding with other festival participants during the event.
Fun Fact: Start a conversation with the quirky history behind this unique celebration of the arts. Originating in 1947, several theater groups entered Scotland’s Edinburgh International Festival and were denied admission. However, these theater groups continued their performances by the “fringe” of the event’s grounds, establishing and inspiring Fringe Festivals around the globe.
