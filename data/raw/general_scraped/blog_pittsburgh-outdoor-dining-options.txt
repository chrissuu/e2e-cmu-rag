Location: 145 Bakery Square Blvd, Pittsburgh, PA 15206
City Kitchen always has plenty of reasons to visit, with four unique restaurants, rotating pop-up concepts and one of the best bars in the city all under one roof in the tech hotspot of Bakery Square .
But the warmer months are even more fun, with City Kitchen's spacious covered patio looking out onto this bustling foodie and shopping epicenter. Be sure to check their events calendar, too, for live music, DJs, family events, yoga and watch parties on the giant outdoor screen.
