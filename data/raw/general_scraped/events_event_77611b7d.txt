Sound Series: Brian Dunne with special guest Dead Gowns
Oct 17, 2025 - Oct 17, 2025																   | 8:00 pm - 10:00 pm
The Warhol Theater 117 Sandusky Street Pittsburgh, PA 15222
We welcome songwriter and guitarist, Brian Dunne for a solo performance in our intimate theater. His 2023 album Loser On The Ropes , named a top record of the year by Rolling Stone, was released on the Kill Rock Stars label and was produced by Drew Vandenberg (Faye Webster, Of Montreal). Dunne is also a member of the band Fantastic Cat, and he has recently toured and shared the stage with artists including Lone Bellow, Cat Power, and Sunny War. Dead Gowns (aka Genevieve Beaudoin) opens the evening.
