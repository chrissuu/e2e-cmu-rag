Pittsburgh Opera's Study Guides are a concise introduction to the opera that includes:
Characters
Synopsis
Biographies
Articles related to the historical context of the opera.
Study Guides are posted in PDF format on each opera's main page prior to the opera's opening, and are available on paper at all performances. Additionally, you can download them below as they become available.
