If you are a CMU faculty or staff member, download and complete the payroll deduction form to make a gift to the university through your paycheck. This form allows you to designate your gift to the college, school, fund or program of your choice through an automatic deduction each month.
Send your completed payroll deduction form to:
Gift Administration Department Carnegie Mellon University Six PPG Place, 11th Floor Pittsburgh, PA 15222
Contact Annual Giving with any questions at annual-giving@andrew.cmu.edu or 412-268-2582 .
