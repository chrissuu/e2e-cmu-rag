By supporting Pittsburgh Opera , you play a critical role in our region’s cultural vitality and growth:
You enrich our city , making Pittsburgh a beacon for world-class opera ranging from traditional favorites to provocative new music , and ensuring the highest level of artistic excellence, even while ticket sales cover less than half the real cost of opera.
You foster rising talent , building opera stars of the future through the Resident Artist Program , ranked in the top-five training programs nationally.
You inspire the next generation , bringing educational workshops and student matinees to 20,000 young people, many of whom are experiencing opera for the very first time.
You create opportunities for everyone , carrying opera into the community with free and approachable performances in diverse neighborhoods throughout our region.
You build a community , meeting and mingling with fellow opera lovers at our Special Events to discover new friends and colleagues.
You join a legacy , uniting with people who have led Pittsburgh Opera from its roots as a female-led organization to its prominence today as the seventh oldest company in the United States.
Pittsburgh Opera is rated Platinum , their highest rating, by industry-leading Guidestar , an information service specializing in reporting on U.S. nonprofit companies.
