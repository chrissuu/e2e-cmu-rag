Notes:
Students taking courses in Heinz College and Tepper School of Business (MBA and
Computational Finance programs) must review and follow separate academic
*
calendars; Similarly, students taking doctoral courses must review and follow the
doctoral calendar for those courses.
Summer Session All corresponds to Summer Semester dates. Summer Session One
** and Summer Session Two correspond to Summer One/Mini-5 and Summer Two/Mini-6
dates and deadlines.
Summer Session All, One and Two final exams will be scheduled by the University
***
Registrar's Office.
Students dropping a course while remaining enrolled (in at least one course-section)
will receive a tuition adjustment only if they drop by this date. Students taking a Leave
1
of Absence or Withdrawing from the university should consult the official Tuition
Adjustment Policy.
Graduate students in CFA, DC and SCS do not receive W grades for course withdrawal.
2
Only graduate students in CIT, HC, MCS and TSB receive W grades.
After this date, course withdrawal is permitted in doctoral course-sections only.
3 Students may not withdraw from undergraduate or master’s course-sections after this
date.
Undergraduate and master’s students may use vouchers to drop, withdraw, or make a
pass/no pass election after the posted deadlines. Doctoral students do not receive
4 vouchers. Advisor approval is required for vouchers to be
initiated, and it is strongly recommended that students contact their advisors before
12 noon on the voucher election deadline date to discuss options.
