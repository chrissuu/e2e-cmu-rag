Ticketholders are invited to attend a Pre-Opera Talk one hour before each performance’s curtain at the Benedum Center, the Byham Theater, and CAPA.
Learn about the composer, the story, and some juicy details about the opera! These talks are free to all ticketholders.
The Pre-Opera Talks are given by Kristin Gatch , Assistant to the General Director and Development Research Associate.
