Diamond Diva Disco
Oct 11, 2025 - Oct 11, 2025																   | 6:00 pm - 9:00 pm
Bitz Opera Factory 2425 Liberty Avenue Pittsburgh, PA 15222
The Diamond Diva Disco kicks off The Pittsburgh Opera's 2025-26 season in style on Saturday, October 11, 2025, at the Bitz Opera Factory.
A 70th annual Diamond Horseshoe Celebration , the Diamond Diva Disco will help you get your groove on with a glamourous evening of opera-disco fusion, plenty of copacetic cocktails, and dancing the night away.
