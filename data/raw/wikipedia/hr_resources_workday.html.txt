Workday
The university's global human resources, payroll, benefits and time tracking system.
Employees can:
Complete the onboarding process
Keep your personal information, such as your address and emergency contacts, up-to-date
View your payslips and update your direct deposit elections
Request PTO and view time off balances
View your benefits elections
Update your benefits elections (during Open Enrollment or life event)
Apply for tuition benefits
Enter time worked (non-exempt employees only)
Maintain your career profile and set up job alerts
Faculty members can also ...
View and monitor your academic appointment information
View and update information if managing other faculty
