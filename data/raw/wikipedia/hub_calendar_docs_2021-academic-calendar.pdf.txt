1. tuition adjustment only if they drop by this date.
-- Students taking a Leave of Absence or Withdrawing from the University should consult the official
Tuition Adjusment Policy

2. TSB students receive W grades for course withdrawal.

ONLY IN THOSE designated course-sections.
