Supporting the Advancement of Women at Carnegie Mellon
The Carnegie Mellon Women's Association (CMWA) celebrates the diversity and achievements of women at Carnegie Mellon while building a greater sense of community life on campus by connecting and supporting members through networking opportunities, social gatherings and educational programming throughout the year.
Membership in the Carnegie Mellon Women's Association (CMWA) is open to all faculty, administrators, trustees, and staff, or spouses/partners and friends of faculty, administrators, trustees, and staff who support the advancement of women in the university community.
