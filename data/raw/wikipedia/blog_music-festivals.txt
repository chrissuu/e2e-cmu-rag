This free, open-air concert is held at Schenley Plaza in Oakland, and features a day-long celebration of national and local artists in a variety of genres.
WYEP also hosts a Neighborhood Concert Series that brings free live music to the Pittsburgh region. From April to September, local musicians and up-and-coming national artists will perform at locations from the South Hills to Monroeville to Oakmont.
