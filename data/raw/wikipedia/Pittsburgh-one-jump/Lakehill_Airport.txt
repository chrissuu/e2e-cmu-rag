Lakehill Airport (FAA LID: P09), was a privately owned airport near Mars, Pennsylvania, U.S., part of the Pittsburgh metropolitan area.  It was the smallest of the three airports located in Butler County.  The remaining two are the Butler County Airport, and the Butler Farm Show Airport.

As of April, 2025, a housing plan is in the process of being built on the former airport property.
