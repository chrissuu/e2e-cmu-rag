Carlos Clark
Bassoon
Paul J. Ross Fellow
Carlos Clark is a bassoonist from Belleville, MI. Along with being principal bassoon of The Atlanta Opera, Carlos plays frequently with orchestras throughout the Midwest and Southeastern U.S.
Mr. Clark studied under William Buchman, Keith Buncke, and Miles Manor of the Chicago Symphony. He also earned his bachelor's degree from Central Michigan University under MaryBeth Minnis.
