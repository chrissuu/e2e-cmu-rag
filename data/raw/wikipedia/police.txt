Welcome to the Carnegie Mellon University Police Department
The Carnegie Mellon University Police Department (CMUPD) is a full-service, community-oriented law enforcement agency committed to protecting and serving the CMU community. We believe public safety is more than just responding to emergencies it is about fostering trust, building partnerships, and working hand-in-hand with the community we serve. Through proactive patrols, crime prevention initiatives, emergency preparedness programs, and campus outreach, we strive to keep Carnegie Mellon University not only secure but also a place where learning and innovation can thrive.
