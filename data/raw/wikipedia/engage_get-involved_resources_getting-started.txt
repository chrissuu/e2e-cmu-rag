Welcome, recent alumni!
The CMU Alumni Association is here for you at every stage.
As a Carnegie Mellon graduate, you are a member of an exclusive group made up of more than 130,000 talented Tartans around the world. The CMU Alumni Association is here to support you as you build the future of your personal and professional life.
The first step is to join the online Alumni Community , the central hub for alumni engagement. There, you can explore upcoming events, networking opportunities, professional development and career support resources and more. You can also connect with the CMU Alumni Association on social media. And, finally, be sure to save the dates for CMU traditions like Homecoming Weekend , Spring Carnival and Reunion .
As you start your journey to take on the world, you can turn to the Tartan community for support.
