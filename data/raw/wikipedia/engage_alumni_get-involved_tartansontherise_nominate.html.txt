The Tartans on the Rise program celebrates recent alumni who are making an impact in their communities, their companies and the world through innovation and leadership.
Please complete the fields below to nominate an alumn as a Tartan on the Rise. Nominees must have received their undergraduate or graduate degree from Carnegie Mellon University within the past 10 years. Graduates from 2015 to 2025 will be recognized in spring 2026.
All nominations received by August 25, 2025, will be considered as 2026 Tartans on the Rise. Nominations received after this date will be considered for future classes.
