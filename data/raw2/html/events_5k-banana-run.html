5K Banana Run

Are you ready to run?

5k Banana Run:

Because running is bananas!

Details

Do you have enough pep in your step to run or walk through historic downtown Latrobe? Make a split for the 5k Banana Run! Whether your running like there’s a delicious banana split in front of you, or you’re up for a sweet stroll, it’s going to be bananas! Keep your eyes peeled on our social media page for additional updates.

  • Saturday, August 22, 2025
  • Registration at 7am, Kickoff at 9am
  • Latrobe Memorial Stadium
