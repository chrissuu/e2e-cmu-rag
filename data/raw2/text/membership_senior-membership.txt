The adventure continues at Carnegie Museums for those aged 65 and over! Seniors save year-round on individual membership to the four Carnegie Museums and enjoy special benefits including invitations to Senior Socials, free year-round admission to four world-class museums, and discounts in our museum shops and cafés.
All senior members enjoy:
Free general admission for one named individual (age 65+) for one year to Carnegie Museum of Art, Carnegie Museum of Natural History, Kamin Science Center, and The Andy Warhol Museum
Invitations to events and programming designed just for teen members
Free reciprocal admission at 300+ ASTC museums and science centers
Discounts on classes, camps, educational films at Rangos Giant Cinema, touring exhibitions, laser shows, lectures, and special events
A 10% discount in museum stores and cafés (20% store discount during Member Shopping Days)
Free subscription to Carnegie magazine
If you’d like to join or renew at a higher level of membership , we offer seniors (65+) a special senior discount on Premium, Family, and Dual memberships . Please call the membership office at 412.622.3314 with any questions.
For individuals and families whose barrier to accessing our museums is primarily a financial one, our $20 Family Access Membership helps remove that barrier.
