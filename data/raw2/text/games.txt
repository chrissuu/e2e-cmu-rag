Pickle Juice Drinking
Contest rules & regulations.
Quench your thirst for brine in this test of speed and fermented fortitude. Whoever can chug a quart (32 fl oz.) of Pittsburgh Pickle Company’s Picklexir the fastest will be deemed the winner of their round and vie for the title of Duke of Drinking in the championship on Sunday.

Bobbing for Pickles
Contest rules & regulations.
No hands are allowed in this pickled take on the peculiar party pastime. Whoever can retrieve the most pickles by weight in 90 seconds using only their mouth will win the round and face-off in the finals to become Picklesburgh’s Baron of Bobbing !

Pickle Eating
Contest rules & regulations.
Only stomachs of steel will prevail in this new contest category! Whoever eats the most pickles by weight in four minutes (‘chipmunking’ is allowed) will win their round and earn a spot in the final, where the Earl of Eating will be crowned.
