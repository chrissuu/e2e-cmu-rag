Entertainment
Milk Bank & Cookies
Nov 6, 2025 - Nov 6, 2025
| 7:00 pm - 10:00 pm
Stage AE
400 North Shore Drive																Pittsburgh, PA 15212
EVENT WEBSITE
The mother of all cookie tables! Don't miss the sweetest night in Pittsburgh, emceed by the legendary Rick Sebak. Regional bakeries will compete to benefit medically fragile babies.
VIP ticket holders will enjoy:
Early entry at 6:00 PM
10 raffle tickets included
Light appetizers before the main event begins
General Admission guests will enter at 7:00 PM for the full cookie competition experience.
All guests will enjoy stunning cookie displays, local celebrity judges, and go home with a box of their favorite cookies as they sample throughout the night.
Proceeds benefit the nonprofit Mid-Atlantic Mothers’ Milk Bank, helping provide lifesaving donor milk to medically fragile infants across PA, WV, NJ, MD, and DE.
