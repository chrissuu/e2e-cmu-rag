Irony City: Am I The Jagoff?
Aug 2, 2025 - Dec 6, 2025
| 8:00 pm - 9:30 pm
Arcade Comedy Theater
943 Liberty Avenue																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Join veteran comedy troupe Irony City as they deftly navigate social missteps on the first Saturday of each month. We’ve all been there: acted out at a wedding when there was no cookie table, ordered fries on a salad at a fancy restaurant, or dared to move a parking chair – afterward wondering, “Am I the Jagoff?”
Each show, the audience shares stories about some real Jagoff moments, sparking hilarious improvised scenes from Irony City.
This event recurs every 1st Saturday of the month until Dec. 6, 2025
