Steel City Horror Show
Oct 26, 2025 - Oct 26, 2025
| 7:30 pm - 9:30 pm
Harris Theater
809 Liberty Ave																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
The monthly Steel City Horror Show returns for another evening of scares and surprises and we expect October's screening to be a doozy!! Last month, we enjoyed (kind of...haha) the cult classic TROLLS 2, and played "Bad Movie Bingo" with the help of the amazing folks at Neo Trash Video!
What will the mystery movie be this time? There's only one way to find out: Be there for the Steel City Horror Show! Host Sean Collier will tell you all about our surprise flick, while the mysterious Dr. Gielgud returns with in-theater scares and pre-show surprises — but which Dr. Gielgud will we meet this time? All this plus a scintillating special guest! (Want a hint? Guests who attended the last show were given a one-word clue, and we'll share it with you, too: "DREAM")
