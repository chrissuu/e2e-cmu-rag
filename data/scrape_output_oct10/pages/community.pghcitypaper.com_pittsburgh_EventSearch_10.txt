The Large Scale Systems Museum (LSSM) will celebrate its 10th anniversary with a special fundraiser weekend in mid-October at 924 4th Avenue, New Kensington, PA. Since 2015, LSSM has been dedicated to preserving and operating historic computer systems from the 1950s through the 1990s, giving visitors the rare chance to experience these machines running as they did decades ago.
The fundraiser will feature live demonstrations of mainframes, minicomputers, supercomputers, and microcomputers, guided ours, special anniversary exhibits, and opportunities to support the museum’s ongoing preservation efforts.
