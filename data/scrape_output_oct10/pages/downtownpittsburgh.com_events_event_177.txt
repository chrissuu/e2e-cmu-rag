Ofrenda Networking Happy Hour
Oct 29, 2025 - Oct 29, 2025
| 5:30 pm - 9:00 am
Greer Cabaret Theater
655 Penn Avenue																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
The Ofrenda Happy Hour, hosted by the Pittsburgh Metropolitan Area Hispanic Chamber of Commerce in partnership with the Pittsburgh Cultural Trust, invites guests to celebrate and connect during a vibrant evening honoring Día de los Muertos. Held at the Greer Cabaret Theater, this event combines cultural tradition with community spirit through an interactive ofrenda — a collaborative altar guided by local artists Alison Zapata and Lisa DiGioia-Nutini, owner of Mexico Lindo. Guests can enjoy festive highlights including a live DJ, themed cocktails, a taco bar, and complimentary Makeup de Muertos artistry. Blending reflection, art, and celebration, the Ofrenda Happy Hour offers an unforgettable way to honor loved ones while embracing the color, rhythm, and warmth of this cherished Mexican tradition.
