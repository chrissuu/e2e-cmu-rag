Steel City Spooky Market
Oct 4, 2025 - Oct 25, 2025
| 10:00 am - 3:00 pm
Frankie Pace Park
901 Centre Ave																Pittsburgh, PA 15219
EVENT WEBSITE
The Steel City Spooky Market is a Halloween themed market - meaning the vendors sell spooky art, tarot readings, taxidermy, crystals, and more. You'll also find food vendors and food trucks that are sometimes themed. We have rotating vendors and are open biweekly, and then weekly in October.
