Yoga in the Square - PPG Plaza Edition!
May 11, 2025 - Oct 12, 2025
| 10:00 am - 11:00 am
PPG Plaza
1 Third Ave																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Pittsburgh’s favorite public yoga series is back! The 2025 season brings some of the city’s best instructors into Downtown for two weekly classes held in the middle of picturesque PPG Plaza. The classes are free, but please bring your own mat!
