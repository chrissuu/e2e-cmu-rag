Festival
Kids Eat Free & Halloween Safety Day w/ UPMC Burn Center
Oct 26, 2025 - Oct 26, 2025
| 3:00 pm - 6:00 pm
Hofbrauhaus Pittsburgh Brewery & Restaurant
2705 S. Water Street																Pittsburgh , PA 15203
EVENT WEBSITE
We’re partnering with BOB-FM and the UPMC Mercy Burn Center—one of the nation’s leading burn care facilities—to support Halloween Safety heading into Trick or Treat Night in our local communities.
Kids Eat Free* w/ purchase of Adult Entree.
UPMC Mercy will be giving away battery-operated tea lights for your pumpkins as well as glow sticks and candy bags for Trick or Treat night.
Plus, BOB-FM will be in the Haus from 3-5PM with a few surprises as well!
? Follow us on social media and tune in to BOB-FM for updates.
