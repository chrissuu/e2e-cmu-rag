Red Bull Turn It Up: Pittsburgh
Oct 17, 2025 - Oct 17, 2025
| 8:00 pm - 10:00 pm
Stage AE
400 N Shore Dr.																Pittsburgh, PA 15219
EVENT WEBSITE
One Theme. One Mix. Who's got the crowd? You Decide! Red Bull Turn It Up is the most entertaining track-selection competition where DJs must create mixes on the spot based on unpredictable themes. The audience votes for the best mix, battle after battle. Each round, the crowd votes for the best mix and the team who gets the room the most hyped-up wins. Finalists will compete in a final 4-round battle for the chance to be crowned the Red Bull Turn It Up Champion!
