Under The Radar
Sep 20, 2025 - Mar 21, 2026
| 7:00 pm - 8:30 pm
Arcade Comedy Theater
943 Liberty Avenue																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Join us for an entertaining night of hilarious comedy at Under The Radar and support Pittsburgh's vibrant improv scene! This monthly showcase of independent teams features a rotating lineup and a supportive atmosphere. You'll experience a dynamic range of comedic styles and performances each month. Don't miss out on the chance to see emerging talent and enjoy an evening of unscripted fun!
