Greenwood Week
Oct 9, 2025 - Oct 11, 2025
Emerald City PGH
213 Smithfield St.																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Greenwood Week is Pittsburgh's only Black Business Conference! It's an event packed with panels, parties, and people that you won't want to miss!
From October 9th to October 11th at Emerald City Pittsburgh, experience educational classes, one-on-one consulting opportunities, collaboration opportunities, cuisine, and meaningful conversations.
Friday is a free co-working day until 4:00 PM. In the evening, there is a State of Black Business.
On Saturday, enjoy a Brunch and day party. People can purchase tickets a la carte or one ticket or all of the events.
