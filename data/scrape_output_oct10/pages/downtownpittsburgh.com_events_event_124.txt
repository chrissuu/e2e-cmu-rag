Symphonie Fantastique
Oct 10, 2025 - Oct 12, 2025
Heinz Hall
600 Penn Avenue																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Dreams turn to demonic nightmares. A march to the scaffold. A church bell tolls. Berlioz’s "Fantasy Symphony” descends from passion into madness across five vivid, soul-baring movements – an audience favorite that’s perfect for fall. Plus, Beethoven at his boldest with his Fourth Piano Concerto, performed by the spellbinding Francesco Piemontesi.
