Bike the Burgh - Beauty of the Burgh Tour
Jan 1, 2025 - Dec 22, 2025
| 10:00 am - 1:00 pm
Bike the Burgh
1049 Penn Ave																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Bike the Burgh's "Best of": On this tour, you will cruise through the streets of the financial and cultural district, explore the Strip District and check out the North Shore. You will find some details that some of you all drove and walked by hundreds of time but probably never really noticed. You will dive into the history of Pittsburgh, tracking down the people who shaped this city to what it is today: a beauty. Old and new buildings, city squares and public art will turn this ride into a unique event full of exciting (re)discoveries of The Burgh.
This tour will also include a fun quiz. The winner will receive a 30% discount on the next tour! Participants will also receive a cute Pittsburgh souvenir.
WHEN? Almost daily at 10 a.m.
PRICE: $49 adults, $34 for children (age 8- 13). Bring your own bike and save $9.
RIDING DISTANCE: 6 miles
TOUR LENGTH: 3 hours
INCLUDED:
- Enthusiastic tour guide who knows his stuff
- Awesome bike + helmet
- Cute Pittsburgh souvenir
- Game with a chance to win 30% off your next tour
TOUR START + END: Bike the Burgh, 1049 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15222
