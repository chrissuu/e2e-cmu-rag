It's October Family Night w/ Coco the Clown!
Oct 14, 2025 - Oct 14, 2025
| 5:00 pm - 8:00 pm
Hofbrauhaus Pittsburgh Brewery & Restaurant
2705 S. Water Street																Pittsburgh , PA 15203
EVENT WEBSITE
Join us for October's Family Night!
Kids Eat FREE* w/ the purchase of an adult entree!
Plus, we've got CoCo The Clown for the kids with face painting and balloon art!
We're also partnering with the UPMC Mercy Burn Center to help raise funds during Fire Prevention Month.
ROUNDING UP your check this October will help fund lifesaving care and recovery for burn patients and at-risk communities through the Copeland Burn Fund.
Don't miss the family fun...and your chance to help such a worthy cause!
PROST!
*Limit one Kids Meal per child.
