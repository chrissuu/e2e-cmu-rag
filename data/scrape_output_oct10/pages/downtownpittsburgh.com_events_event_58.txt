Halloween Bar Crawls 2025
Oct 25, 2025 - Oct 31, 2025
| 5:00 pm - 11:30 pm
Twelve Whiskey Barbecue
1222 East Carson Street																Pittsburgh, PA 15203
EVENT WEBSITE
Dress up. Link up. Drink up. Join us for the 4th Annual Ghouls Gone Wild, Pittsburgh’s premier Halloween bar crawl experience. This is not your average crawl - this is a crazy night of thrills, cocktails, music, and style across 5+ of the best venues in Pittsburgh's South Side District.
Whether you're channeling vintage glam, dark fantasy, or classic horror, this is your night to show up and stand out.
One Ticket. One District. Multiple Bars. Endless Vibes.
Your ticket unlocks access to all participating Bars - each offering its own atmosphere, crowd, and signature drink experiences.
MANDATORY CHECK-IN BETWEEN 5PM - 7PM: Twelve Whiskey Barbecue - 1222 E Casron St.
____
WHAT'S INCLUDED- Five Complimentary Welcome Shots – Redeem at the bars of your choice (limit 1 per venue)- Signature Drink Specials – Curated for the occasion- Free Cover – At all participating venues (no re-entry at some locations)- Access to After Parties – Keep the night going- Professional Event Photography – You bring the look, we’ll capture the moment- Halloween Treats – Trick-or-treating isn’t just for kids- Costume Contest – Post your look, tag us, and win- Social Energy & Shared Madness – This night is meant to be lived out loud
EVENT PERKS:Share with your friends - Save 20% on orders of 4 or more!Get your tickets now - Take advantage of Early Bird discounts.
For full event details, including all participating bars, schedule updates and more, please visit our ticket page. Don’t miss out on the ultimate Halloween Bar Crawl fun! ? ?
