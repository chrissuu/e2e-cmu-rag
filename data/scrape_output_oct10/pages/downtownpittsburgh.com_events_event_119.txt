Haunted Three Rivers Cruise
Oct 3, 2025 - Oct 25, 2025
| 6:30 pm - 7:30 pm
Gateway Clipper Fleet
350 West Station Square Drive																Pittsburgh, PA 15219
EVENT WEBSITE
Step aboard the Haunted Three Rivers Cruise, where the rivers come alive with chilling tales of ghosts and mysteries that will send shivers down your spine. Explore haunted bridges, spectral captains, and ghostly apparitions as history and mystery converge on Pittsburgh’s darkened waterways.
Prepare for a night of chills, thrills, and unforgettable scares.
Haunted Three Rivers Cruise Details:Enjoy a spooky-themed cruise complete with a live storyteller and a few surprises along the way. A full-service bar is available on board — ask the bartenders for the weekly special! Must be 21+ with valid photo ID to consume alcoholic beverages. (NO CASH ON BOARD – Credit/Debit cards, Google Pay, and Apple Pay accepted.) A photo team will capture boarding pictures of each group, available for purchase day-of or pre-purchase during checkout. A snack bar will also be open for onboard purchases.
