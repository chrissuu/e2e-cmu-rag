A beloved Pittsburgh tradition that brings K–12 students into the world of orchestral music—free for all schools, in person and online.
Schooltime @ Heinz Hall

Digital Schooltime
Bring the Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra to your classroom! Digital Schooltime is free for educators and includes units for every grade level with concert videos and teacher resources, all available online.
