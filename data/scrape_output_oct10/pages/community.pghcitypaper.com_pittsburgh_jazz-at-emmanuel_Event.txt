An ecumenical jazz service, free and open to all.
Swinging jazz mixed with the spoken word.
This month's theme is Harvest.
Musicians in include Ronni Weiss – vocals, Scott Boni - alto saxophone, Mark Strickland - guitar, Leon Boykins - bass, Thomas Wendt - drums and musical director.
