Stand-Up Laboratory
Sep 4, 2025 - Dec 4, 2025
| 9:00 pm - 10:30 pm
Arcade Comedy Theater
943 Liberty Ave																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
The Stand-Up Laboratory is an incubator space presented by Arcade Comedy Theater. Every month, the Lab will present a mix of feature comics and a roster of comedians chosen from online submissions. Enjoy a night of comedy highlighting newer material from Pittsburgh's best comedians and its rising stars.
Happens on the following Dates:
Sep 4, 2025, 9:00pm to 10:30pm Timezone: EDT
Oct 2, 2025, 9:00pm to 10:30pm Timezone: EDT
Nov 6, 2025, 9:00pm to 10:30pm Timezone: EDT
Dec 4, 2025, 9:00pm to 10:30pm Timezone: EDT
