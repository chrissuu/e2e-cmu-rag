Ghouls Night at the Women Who Rock Shop at SouthSide Works
Oct 25, 2025 - Oct 25, 2025
| 5:00 pm - 8:00 pm
Women Who Rock Pop Up Shop - Southside Works
517 S 27th Street																Pittsburgh, PA 15203
EVENT WEBSITE
What to Expect:
Bedazzle Your WWR Pink Pumpkin at The WWR Shop (All Ages): Bring your pumpkin from the Great Pink Pumpkin Patch (open from 2 PM in SouthSide Works Town Square) and give it a glam makeover in-store!
Exclusive Halloween Apparel: Shop our limited-edition Women Who Rock Halloween collection.
Flash Tattoos & Permanent Jewelry: Enjoy custom designs by Inked n Linked, inspired by Breast Cancer Awareness Month, Women Who Rock, and Halloween.
Treats: Enjoy complimentary mocktails and Pittsburgh Popcorn
In-Store Soundtrack by the Legendary Hallow-Queens: The perfect playlist to set the spooky-chic vibe.
Support Women’s Health: Purchase your WWR Pink Pumpkin right outside the shop or in SSW Town Square to benefit Magee-Womens Research Institute. Donations can also be made via QR code inside the shop.
Cost: Free to attend.
Pink Pumpkin purchases directly benefit breast cancer and women’s health research at Magee-Womens Research Institute (501c3).
This event happens in conjunction with the Neighborhood Flea Night Market at SouthSide Works featuring local small businesses, vintage sellers, collectors, food trucks, local services, and artists.
