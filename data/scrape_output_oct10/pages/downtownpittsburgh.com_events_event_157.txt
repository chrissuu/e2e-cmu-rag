Sidra Bell
Oct 17, 2025 - Oct 17, 2025
| 7:00 pm - 9:00 pm
Pittsburgh Playhouse
350 Forbes Ave.																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Experience the groundbreaking artistry of Sidra Bell Dance New York at the Pittsburgh Playhouse, where innovation meets emotional depth in a mesmerizing evening of contemporary dance. As part of this dynamic performance, Point Park University dance students and alumni will take the stage in a special collaboration, bringing Bell’s visionary choreography to life. Don’t miss this unique fusion of professional excellence and emerging talent in a performance that pushes the boundaries of movement and storytelling.
