Fall Dance Concert
Oct 8, 2025 - Oct 12, 2025
Pittsburgh Playhouse
350 Forbes Ave.																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
The Conservatory Dance Company presents a fall concert featuring a diverse range of ballet, modern, jazz, hip hop, and contemporary works choreographed by established and emerging guest artists and a faculty of renowned dance professionals. The 2025 Fall Dance Concert will feature works by Jae Man Joo (Ballet), Martha Graham (Modern), Sidra Bell (Contemporary), and Anthony Thomas (Hip Hop/Jazz Funk).
Performance Times:
Wednesday, October 8, 2025 at 7:30 PM
Thursday, October 9, 2025 at 7:30 PM
Friday, October 10, 2025 at 7:30 PM
Saturday, October 11, 2025 at 2:00 PM
Saturday, October 11, 2025 at 7:30 PM
Sunday, October 12, 2025 at 2:00 PM
