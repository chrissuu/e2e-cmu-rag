Bikes & Brews Tour
Jun 28, 2025 - Dec 28, 2025
| 2:00 pm - 5:30 pm
Bike the Burgh
1049 Penn Ave																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Join Bike the Burgh on a ride through the historic districts of Deutschtown, Millvale, and Lawrenceville, where they will visit four fan-favorite local breweries. Learn about the past, present, and future of beer-making in Pittsburgh, with an emphasis on how beer figures in the diverse communities of the Steel City.
Your tour will be packed with a wealth of history, fascinating facts about beer making, and beer samples from each of the places we visit. Bring your own bike (and lock) and save $9!
Requirements: 21+ and bring your ID!
Duration: ~3.5 hours, 9 miles
Price: $69 (Includes an enthusiastic tour guide, awesome bike + helmet, local food and the visit of 4 breweries.)
