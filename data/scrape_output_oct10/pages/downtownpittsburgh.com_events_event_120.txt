Boo N Brew Halloween Dance Cruise
Oct 25, 2025 - Oct 25, 2025
| 8:00 pm - 10:30 pm
Gateway Clipper Fleet
350 West Station Square Drive																Pittsburgh, PA 15219
EVENT WEBSITE
Arrive at the dock starting at 6:00 pm for free psychic and tarot card readings, prizes, and the chance to mingle with BOB FM on-air personalities. At 8:00 pm, the party hits the water with the band “412 Party Wave” playing all the favorite hits – 96.9 BOB FM style! Don’t forget to wear a Halloween costume for the chance to win prizes including concert and event tickets, plus a fantastic grand prize to be announced.
Must be 21 years of age to board the boat. Valid photo ID required.
