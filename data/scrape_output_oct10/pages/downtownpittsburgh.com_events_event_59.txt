Dia de los Muertos
Oct 31, 2025 - Nov 1, 2025
| 7:00 pm - 12:00 am
Rebel Room
405 Wood Street																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Spirits in our glass to honor the spirits that visit us tonight...
The veil between worlds grows thin this Halloween weekend, and the Rebel Room beckons you into a haunting celebration with a sinister south-of-the-border twist! Their cursed kitchen conjures up spine-chilling dishes while our bartenders brew bewitching Casamigos cocktail concoctions that will raise the dead. Ghostly music echoes through our macabre décor as shadows dance in every corner.
Don your most spectacular Día de los Muertos attire and prepare for bone-rattling surprises throughout the night. But beware – restless spirits roam our halls, and you might find yourself sharing a drink with the dearly departed. Will you be brave enough to join the living... and the undead... for a night of otherworldly fun?
