Black & Ghost Tour
Apr 1, 2025 - Dec 30, 2025
| 7:00 pm - 9:00 pm
Walk the Burgh Tours
1049 Penn Ave																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
A history tour with a spooky twist! Experience the spookiest of Pittsburgh’s Downtown! Get to see outstanding historic landmarks (often beautifully lit up in the darker and colder months) during the quiet evening hours and learn about their history and which spirits lurk in them. Don't worry about picking up any ghostly travelers; the entirety of the tour takes place outdoors! Ready to get chills and be scared by some obscure ghost stories? If so, click below to see which dates are available for this chilling experience! - Kids under the age of 6 and dogs welcome on tour with advance notice.
