2025 Clean Currents Annual Tradeshow
Oct 14, 2025 - Oct 17, 2025
David L. Lawrence Convention Center
1000 Fort Duquesne Blvd																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
With a focus on fostering collaboration, driving innovation, and propelling the industry towards a sustainable and clean energy future, this event serves as a pivotal platform for advancing the waterpower sector.
The Pittsburgh region, nestled among three rivers, is a growing powerhouse for water power production, with innovative projects coming online bringing steady, sustainable power to residents and businesses. With rivers and reservoirs galore, Pittsburgh is the perfect setting for your visit to Clean Currents.
Designed with industry input and driven by industry needs, proceeds from Clean Currents will fund NHA’s work to secure water power’s place as a climate-friendly, renewable, and reliable energy source that serves national environmental, energy, and economic policy objectives. At Clean Currents, you’ll see that Hydro is heading toward a more sustainable future for the industry!
