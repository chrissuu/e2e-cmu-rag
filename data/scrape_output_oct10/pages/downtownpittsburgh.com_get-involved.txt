Get Involved
There are plenty of ways to get involved in Downtown Pittsburgh. From becoming a member, to volunteering, or being a vendor to thousands of Downtowners, the limits are endless! Take a look around to explore how you can be a part of our efforts. Have questions about other ways to get involved? Contact us.
Membership
Members live, work, and play Downtown and are interested in creating an atmosphere where businesses grow. As a member of the Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership, you will have many opportunities to grow your business and make an impact on the country’s “Most Livable City.”
Employment Opportunities
Learn about joining the Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership team.
Volunteer
Rewarding and fun, volunteering with the Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership Clean Team helps keep Downtown vibrant while showing off your civic pride.
Become a Sponsor
Partnering with the Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership offers a wide range of highly visible opportunities to reach the Downtown business, civic, professional, and residential communities. Through PDP events and programming to research, reports, and member promotions, learn more about sponsoring.
Vendor Opportunities
The Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership offers many opportunities for local vendors, artisans, farmers, and community groups to get involved in and showcase their offerings to our diverse Downtown community.
Apply to Host an Event
If you are looking to host an event in Market Square please fill out this application. To host an event in Market Square, you first need to acquire a Special Event Permit from the City of Pittsburgh. Once you have obtained an approved permit, please complete this application. (Note: submitting a complete application does not guarantee approval. Applicants will be notified of approval by email.)
Downtown Community Forums
Get engaged with your Downtown community:
learn about efforts to ensure Downtown Pittsburgh remains a great place to live, work, and play. Share your experiences; ask questions about construction, development, and placemaking initiatives; and discuss the happenings that affect the quality of life and that are making Downtown an essential neighborhood for the region.
Downtown Development Activities Meeting
Offered as a way for residents, business owners, property owners, and more to provide feedback to project leaders, the Downtown Development Activities Meetings allow neighborhood stakeholders to hear about plans proposed to affect the Downtown community.
