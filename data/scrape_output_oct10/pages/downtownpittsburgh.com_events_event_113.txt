2025 ATA Fall Nationals Tournament
Oct 22, 2025 - Oct 25, 2025
David L. Lawrence Convention Center
1000 Fort Duquesne Blvd																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
The American Taekwondo Association brings competitors from across the country together for the 2025 Fall Nationals Tournament. Athletes of all ages and skill levels will showcase their discipline, strength, and spirit in a weekend of competition, camaraderie, and celebration of martial arts. Join fellow practitioners, families, and fans for this exciting national event.
