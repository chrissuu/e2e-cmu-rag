Building Bridges, Building Trails - a Fundraiser for Friends of the Riverfront
Love the trails? Raise a glass to support them!
Add to iCal
Add to Google Calendar
Add to Outlook
When:
Tue., Oct. 21, 6-9 p.m.
Get Tickets
$65
A fundraiser for our trails! Keynote speaker: Sara Innamorato. Craft brews from Abstract Realm and New France breweries and catered by Comfort and Spice. This event is for everyone who supports our riverfront trails, the Three Rivers Heritage Trail and Water Trail. Awards to be presented to Duquesne Light and Chris Blackwell. Auction and raffle of local merch, food/drink and experiences. Take in rooftop views while connecting with the trail-supporting community and supporting the expansion of our trails - including the forthcoming Hazelwood to Carrie Furnace segment!
