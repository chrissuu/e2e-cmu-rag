Trick or Treating at Museum of Illusions Pittsburgh
Oct 27, 2025 - Oct 31, 2025
| 10:00 am - 9:00 pm
Museum of Illusions Pittsburgh
267 N Shore Dr																Pittsburgh, PA 15212
EVENT WEBSITE
Museum of Illusions Pittsburgh adds a playful Halloween twist this season! From Monday, October 27 through Friday, October 31, 2025, families can enjoy early trick-or-treating. Kids will collect treats while exploring mind-bending exhibits like Head on a Plate, the Ames Room, and the Vortex Tunnel, perfect for a spooky and mind-bending adventure.
