What Is "Best Of PGH"?
Pittsburgh City Paper's "Best of PGH" issue celebrates the most influential, underrated, dynamic, and all-around best businesses, creators, events, and leaders in our community. We team up with our readers to unearth hidden gems and reward old faithfuls, bringing you a jam-packed issue that serves as your key to the city all year round.üèÜ‚ú®
