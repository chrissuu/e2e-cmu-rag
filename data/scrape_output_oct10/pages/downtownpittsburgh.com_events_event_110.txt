21+ Night: Monster Murder Mystery
Oct 10, 2025 - Oct 10, 2025
| 6:00 pm - 10:00 pm
Kamin Science Center
1 Allegheny Avenue																Pittsburgh, PA 15212
EVENT WEBSITE
Use real forensic techniques to find the culprit while celebrating all things spooky season at Kamin Science Center. Come in costume and enjoy lots of treats!
Sip on some spooktacular themed cocktails!
Roam of all five floors of the museum kid-free!
Try out some forensic methods, like fiber analysis, toxicity, and fingerprinting.
Catch a Halloween-themed science show in one of our theaters!
Compare what you’ve seen on TV to real life Crime Scene Investigation (CSI) procedures with Dr. Lyndsie Ferrara of Duquesne University.
Learn about the origins of monster lore from Dr. Marcie Persyn of University of Pittsburgh.
Demo some horror games with local video game developer MegaCat Studios, known for “Five Nights at Freddy’s: Into the Pit!”
