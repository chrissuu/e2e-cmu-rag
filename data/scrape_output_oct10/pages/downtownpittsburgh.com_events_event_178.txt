Convention
Clean Currents 2025
Oct 14, 2025 - Oct 17, 2025
David L. Lawrence Convention Center
1000 Fort Duquesne Blvd																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Head to the City of Bridges to make water power connections that can transform your career, your operation, and the industry as a whole. Clean Currents 2025 will take place in Pittsburgh, PA — a region nestled among three rivers and emerging as a growing powerhouse for water power production. Innovative projects across the area are delivering steady, sustainable energy to residents and businesses alike. With its abundance of rivers and reservoirs, Pittsburgh provides the perfect backdrop for this year’s event.
Attendees will have the opportunity to explore what’s new and notable in hydropower and marine energy, learning directly from subject matter experts, leading power producers, and dynamic suppliers driving innovation in the field.
