PGH2050 Public Workshop
Oct 22, 2025 - Oct 23, 2025
| 5:00 pm - 7:30 pm
Energy Innovation Center
1435 Bedford Ave																Pittsburgh, AL 15219
EVENT WEBSITE
Pittsburgh residents are invited to join us for a Comprehensive Plan Public Workshop this October!
Come share your voice and help shape how our city grows and develops in the years ahead. Two sessions will be held — October 22 and October 23, from 5:00 to 7:30 PM, at the Energy Innovation Center (1435 Bedford Avenue). You’ve already helped identify Pittsburgh’s priorities — now it’s time to talk about how we bring them to life through development. Your ideas and participation will help build a stronger, more connected Pittsburgh for everyone!
RSVP here: https://pgh2050.com/upcomingevents
