Plant Nite: Pick Your Own Planter!
Oct 27, 2025 - Oct 27, 2025
| 6:30 pm - 8:00 pm
Aslin Beer Co
1801 Smallman St																Pittsburgh, PA 15222
EVENT WEBSITE
Join Aslin Beer Co in Pittsburgh for an evening of creativity and fun with Plant Nite featuring Katie Casey!
Grab a drink and get ready to dig in as Katie guides you step-by-step in creating your own beautiful succulent terrarium. No green thumb? No problem! Katie will provide all the supplies and expert tips to help your plants thrive. Plus, Aslin Beer Co. offers a delicious selection of craft beers to enjoy while you create. So gather your friends and join us for a night of laughter, drinks, and succulent success!
