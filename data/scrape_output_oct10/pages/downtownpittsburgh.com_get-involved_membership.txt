Membership
Our members are a diverse network of stakeholders who share in our vision for Downtown and the region as a whole.
From the ever-expanding popularity of Picklesburgh, to the bustling outdoor dining scene, to dedicated services that keep our streets safe and clean, our mission is to foster a Downtown that is welcoming to all, and our members are vital part of everything we do.

Accomplishments and Priorities
The PDP has a long history of providing innovative programs and services to improve and enhance Downtown’s business environment, which includes:
Programming public spaces and producing events to keep Downtown vibrant and activeEncouraging alternative modes of transportation and improving pedestrian and bike safety DowntownReducing quality-of-life issues by providing outreach services to the homeless and individuals in needAdvocating for transportation funding solutions via the KeepPGHMoving campaignPartnering with the City of Pittsburgh to ensure Market Square becomes a premier public space for Downtown workers, residents, and visitorsPromoting enhancements to the built environment through the Paris to Pittsburgh streetscape and façade improvement programConducting Downtown-focused research to promote the successes of Downtown and encourage investments to ensure Downtown remains the commercial and cultural hub for the region.
What Our Members Have to Say:
“The assistance provided by the Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership has been instrumental in our redevelopment efforts of the Union Trust Building. This has allowed us to recruit new tenants and several new restaurants to this historic Downtown landmark. Their research, data collection, and expertise help us to make a compelling case for investing in Downtown.”Christopher Lasky, Vice President of Development, The Davis Companies
“As naming sponsor of Light Up Night, Comcast has found the Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership to be a great asset and partner, an organization that works promptly and professionally to help us reach our goals and make the city an outstanding place to do business. The visibility we have achieved with PDP’s help and experience has exceeded our expectations and we look forwrad to continuing our relationship.”Christine Whitaker, Senior Vice President, Comcast
