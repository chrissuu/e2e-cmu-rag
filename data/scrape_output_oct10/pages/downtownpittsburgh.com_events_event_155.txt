About the Show
Lysistrata: A Woman's Translation
By Drue Robinson
The women of Greece have had enough. With daring humor, Aristophanes’ classic comedy follows the fearless Lysistrata as she leads a rebellion like no other, proving that love, laughter and a little rebellion can change the world.
