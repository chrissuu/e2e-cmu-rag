Three Rivers Sightseeing Cruise
Apr 5, 2025 - Nov 1, 2025
Gateway Clipper Fleet Docks
350 West Station Square Dr																Pittsburgh, PA 15219
EVENT WEBSITE
The only way to see the City of Pittsburgh and truly learn about her past, present and future is aboard one of the Gateway Clipper's Sightseeing Cruises. Your Pittsburgh riverboat tour will begin on the Monongahela River and will continue on both the Allegheny and Ohio Rivers, all while the Captain and Narrator relay interesting facts, tales and river lore that make Pittsburgh and Western Pennsylvania so special!
Food & Beverage Service
• Click here for the Snack Bar Menu
• Sodas, beer, wine & liquor are available for purchase. Must be 21 years of age with valid photo identification to purchase alcoholic beverages
What's included
• Experience a One Hour Tour sailing the Allegheny, Monongahela and Ohio Rivers.
• Enjoy Insightful Narration about Pittsburgh and the Three Rivers from our Captain and On-Board Narrator.
• Have a Tasty Snack or a Cool Drink from our Snack Bar and Full Service Bar available for purchase.
